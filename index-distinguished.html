01 Professional Summary

Designing how organizations build software with agents.

I design the systems and platforms that let organizations treat AI agents as first-class participants in the engineering process. My focus is the architecture beneath AI-native delivery: how agents plan, execute and verify work; how context flows between humans and models; and how enterprises adopt autonomous engineering without surrendering governance or quality.

Over the past year I have led the design of agent orchestration platforms, an Agentic SDLC, and the evaluation and human-in-the-loop frameworks that make autonomous engineering trustworthy at scale. I work across the full arc — from experimentation and reference architecture to platform engineering and executive strategy — translating where the frontier is heading into systems teams can actually run.

The throughline of my career is the same problem viewed at successive depths: building architectures that let large engineering organizations move faster without breaking — first through modular systems and platforms, now through autonomous and multi-agent ones.
